# Next14 Metapath Position

## Paper Position

Metapath/path-mass diagnostics are appendix-only in Next14. They measure typed
transition mass along schema paths, not held-out fused low-frequency operator
preservation.

## Interpretation

Next13 path-mass results were method-sensitive, but they did not support P/S
superiority over flatten-sum or H6-no-spec. Therefore path-mass should not be
used as main evidence for HeSF-LVC-P/S.

The safe interpretation is:

- path-mass can show secondary behavior against weaker baselines such as H0,
  random, GraphZoom-style, ConvMatch-style, and TypedHash;
- path-mass does not establish that P/S preserve metapaths better than
  flatten-sum/H6;
- the main preservation evidence should remain `paper_final_dee`,
  `relation_energy_error`, and the held-out fused-operator probe when it
  supports the claim.
